The Difference

What Changes When You Use InboxRoute

Without InboxRoute
  • ✕
    DMs go unanswered for hours

    Prospects message you and hear nothing. By the time you reply, they've already moved on.

  • ✕
    Generic, robotic auto-replies

    Basic bots that say "Thanks for reaching out!" and nothing else. Kills trust immediately.

  • ✕
    Pricing questions go nowhere

    Leads ask about cost or availability and never get a real answer. They book with a competitor.

  • ✕
    No clear path to booking

    Even interested leads have to chase you for a booking link. Most don't bother.

With InboxRoute
  • ✓
    Instant replies, every time

    Every inbound DM gets an immediate, intelligent response — day or night, weekend or holiday.

  • ✓
    Sounds exactly like your team

    Trained on your services and voice. Prospects feel like they're talking to a real person on your staff.

  • ✓
    Handles pricing & objections

    Answers specific questions about cost, availability, and details using your custom knowledge base.

  • ✓
    Routes straight to booking

    The moment a lead is ready, they get your booking link automatically. No friction, no follow-up needed.
