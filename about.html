About InboxRoute

Why We Built InboxRoute

Nathan and Daniel met working the same job in high school. They stayed friends into college, both ended up at Illinois State University, became roommates, and spent a lot of time talking about business ideas.

We kept running into the same wall. Reach out to a business on Instagram or Facebook, wait two days, hear nothing. Or get a reply days later when you've already moved on. It was frustrating every single time.

We figured we were not the only ones experiencing this. Turns out, we were right.

On the business side, it made sense too. Coaches, fitness studios, real estate agents, consultants, they were getting flooded with DMs and had no real system to handle them. They weren't ignoring people on purpose. They just didn't have the bandwidth. Every missed message was a missed opportunity.

So we built InboxRoute at the start of 2026. Fully managed AI automation that handles DMs, qualifies leads, and books calls — so business owners can focus on actually running their business.

Our first client booked three appointments in their first week.

That told us everything we needed to know.
